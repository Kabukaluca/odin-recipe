Creamy Spicy Pasta

Discription:

This is recipe for real Pasta Lovers!

It is the perfect creamy but spicy cheese sauce! but yet fast to make Pasta

Ingridients:

  • 1-2 Red Onions
  • Chilli
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • Tomato Paste
  • Heavy Cream
  • Basil
  • 1 Mozarella
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil your pasta as we countinue with the next steps
  2. Cut onions, garlic, chilli and mozarella.
  3. Fry onions, garlic and chilli until onions glassy
  4. Add tomato paste, mix and fry for another minute
  5. Now add your heavy cream and stir until mixed
  6. Add the mozarella cheese and mix with the sauce
  7. Add your pasta
  8. To finnish put on a plate and serve with fresh basil
